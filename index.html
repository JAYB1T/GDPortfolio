About Me

My Journey into Game Development

The Beginning

Games have been a part of my life since I was a child, and I have always dreamed of creating my own games. The first game I ever played was Super Mario 64 DS, and it sparked my love for gaming. I remember spending hours exploring the Mushroom Kingdom, jumping, and stomping goombas. I loved the sense of adventure and discovery that the game offered. I was fascinated by how the game was made and how it created such an immersive experience.

As I grew up, I started to explore more games and genres. I played everything from platformers to RPGs, and I was always amazed by the creativity and innovation in game design. I remember playing games like Red Dead Redemption and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, which had rich worlds and incredibly fun gameplay. I was particularly drawn to the storytelling and world-building aspects of these games. These games influenced my passion for game design and development, and I knew I wanted to create my own games one day.

My Development Journey

I began my development journey in 2019 watching tutorials and building small demos with Unity. I would use basic blocks to play around and prototype. My first actual experience with projects was in my Intro to Interactive Media class where I had to build a few projects using Scratch. I currently have experience in Unity and C#, and I recently started exploring Unreal Engine 5. I'm also an officer in the UMD Game Developers Club, where I collaborate with other students to share knowledge. I am always looking to learn new things and improve my skills in game development.

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Information Science with a minor in Technology Innovation Leadership from the University of Maryland, College Park. This has given me a solid foundation in technology and problem-solving. I also gained an analytical mindset through my coursework, which I apply to my game development projects.

I have a strong interest in game design, level design, combat design, and world building. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a game developer. I am currently working on more projects that showcase my skills and creativity in game design and development.

Beyond Development

In my free time, I enjoy playing games, reading and watching videos about game design, and exploring new technologies. I'm passionate about understanding what makes games engaging and memorable, which drives me to constantly learn and experiment with new ideas.

Beyond gaming, I'm an avid reader with interests spanning multiple genres. Reading helps me understand narrative structure, character development, and world-building techniques that I apply to my game design work. I also enjoy staying active, exploring creative hobbies, and spending time with friends and family.
